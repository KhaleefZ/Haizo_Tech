How we work

Engineering you can inspect

No black boxes. You get the repository, the deployment pipeline and the documentation from day one — because the point is that you can keep running this after we've finished.

RBAC

Role-based access enforced server-side on every protected route, not hidden in the UI.

Encryption

Secrets in environment stores, hashed credentials, TLS everywhere, parameterised queries only.

GDPR / DPDP

Consent capture, retention limits, and no personal data in plaintext logs.

CI/CD

Typecheck, lint, build and test gate every merge. The rollback path is known before we deploy.

Process

Development process

The same four steps on every engagement, whether it’s a six-week build or a long-running retainer.

01

Research & Planning

Understanding constraints and requirements.

We map the problem, the users and the limits you actually operate under, then write down what v1 will and won’t include.

02

Design & Prototype

System design and technical foundation.

Flows and screens you can click, and the data model underneath them, settled before anything is expensive to change.

03

Development & Testing

Iterative sprint execution and QA.

Weekly working software on a staging URL you can open. Typecheck, lint and tests gate every merge.

04

Deployment & Maintenance

Staging to production rollout.

We deploy, watch it, hand over the keys and stay on through the settling-in period.
