Everything on this page reads from a nightly rollup, not from raw events. A scheduled job folds the previous day's page views, enquiries and reply timings into one MetricDaily row per day per dimension (path, source, service). The dashboard then reads at most a few hundred rows for any range, so it answers in the same few milliseconds whether the site has a month of history or five years of it. Querying the raw page-view table directly would look identical today and get slower every week. The cost of the rollup is that today's numbers only appear after the job runs — which is why the range control never offers "today".
