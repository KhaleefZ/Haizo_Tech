Questions

What people ask before signing

Who owns the code?

You do, from the first commit. The repository sits in your organisation and we work in it — not in ours, with a handover promised later. The same goes for the domain, the cloud accounts and the database.

What does it cost, roughly?

Most custom v1 builds land between ₹5L and ₹15L, driven mainly by the number of roles and integrations. We quote a fixed price per phase after scoping, so you're never approving an open-ended meter.

Can you work on our existing system?

Yes, and it's a fair amount of what we do. We start with a read-only review of the codebase and infrastructure, then give you a written assessment before proposing work — including the option that says leave it alone.

What happens after launch?

We stay on through the settling-in period, when the real usage patterns show up. After that you can keep a monthly support arrangement or take it fully in-house — the documentation and pipeline are built assuming you'll do the latter.

Tell us what's breaking

A short call is usually enough for us to say whether custom software is the answer — or whether something cheaper would do.
