Privacy policy

This policy explains what personal data HaizoTech collects through this website, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and what you can ask us to do with it.

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Who we are

HaizoTech is a software engineering studio based in Coimbatore, India. For the personal data described below, we are the data fiduciary under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (the DPDP Act) and the data controller under the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), where that applies to you.

You can reach us at info@haizotech.com or +91 8807341655 about anything in this policy.

What we collect

Information you give us

If you submit the contact or project form, we collect what you type into it:

  • Name — so we know who we're replying to.
  • Email address — the address we reply to.
  • Phone number — used only if you'd prefer a call, or if email bounces.
  • Company name — so we can understand the context of your enquiry.
  • Your message — the description of what you want built.

Please don't put sensitive information — credentials, customer records, financial or health data — into that form. If you need to share something confidential, tell us and we'll arrange a secure channel first.

Information collected automatically

We keep basic analytics about how the site is used: pages viewed, approximate region, referring site, device type and browser. This is aggregate traffic measurement — it tells us which pages are useful, not who you are. We do not build advertising profiles and we do not run cross-site tracking.

Why we use it, and on what legal basis

  • To answer your enquiry and scope work. Basis: your consent when you submit the form, and, once a project is under discussion, the necessity of taking steps at your request before entering a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and 6(1)(b)). Under the DPDP Act, this is processing for the specified purpose you consented to.
  • To deliver and support work you've engaged us for. Basis: performance of our contract with you.
  • To keep records we're required to keep — invoices and tax records, for example. Basis: our legal obligation.
  • To understand and improve how the site performs. Basis: our legitimate interest in running a functioning website, using data that does not identify you individually.

We do not use your enquiry to add you to a marketing list. If we ever start a newsletter, it will be a separate, explicit opt-in.

How long we keep it

  • Enquiries that don't become projects: kept for up to 24 months, then deleted, so we have context if you come back to us.
  • Client project records: kept for the life of the engagement and for as long afterwards as we're required to keep contractual and tax records.
  • Analytics: retained in aggregate only.

You can ask us to delete your data sooner — see “Your rights” below.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data, and we never have. We don't rent it, trade it, or hand it to advertising networks.

We share it only with:

  • Service providers who run our tooling — email, hosting and analytics. They process data on our instructions under a written agreement, and for no other purpose.
  • Authorities, where the law requires it — and only to the extent it genuinely requires it.

Some of those providers operate outside India. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on the transfer safeguards available under the applicable law — including standard contractual clauses for transfers out of the EEA or UK.

Cookies

We use the minimum we can. Strictly necessary cookies keep the site working — for example, remembering that you've dismissed a notice. Analytics cookies, where used, measure traffic in aggregate and are set only with your consent where consent is required.

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the site; blocking analytics cookies will not.

Your rights

Depending on where you are, you have some or all of the following rights:

  • Access — ask what personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — have inaccurate or incomplete data fixed.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete data we no longer need to keep.
  • Withdraw consent — where we relied on consent, withdraw it at any time. This doesn't undo processing already carried out lawfully.
  • Object or restrict — object to processing based on legitimate interests, or ask us to pause processing while a dispute is resolved (GDPR).
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (GDPR).
  • Nominate — nominate another person to exercise your rights on your behalf if you are unable to (DPDP Act).
  • Complain — to the Data Protection Board of India, or to your local supervisory authority in the EEA or UK.

How to make a request

Email info@haizotech.com with what you'd like us to do. Say clearly which right you're exercising and give us enough detail to find your records — usually the email address you contacted us from is enough.

We'll acknowledge your request and respond within 30 days. If a request is unusually complex we'll tell you, explain why, and give you a revised timeline. We won't charge you for a reasonable request. If we have to refuse one, we'll tell you why and how to challenge that.

Keeping data secure

Form submissions travel over TLS, access to the systems holding them is limited to the people who need it, and credentials are stored in a secrets manager rather than in code. No system is perfectly secure, but if a breach affected your personal data we would notify you and the relevant authority as the law requires.

Children

This site is aimed at businesses and is not directed at children. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has sent us personal data, contact us and we'll delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle personal data, we'll update this page and change the “last updated” date. Material changes will be flagged clearly rather than made quietly.
