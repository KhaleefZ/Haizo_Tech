About us

A small team that ships

HaizoTech is a software services agency in Coimbatore. We take on a small number of engagements at a time so that the people who scoped your project are the ones who build it.

Our story

Built here, on purpose

We started in 2023 because too much software sold to Indian businesses is either a template bent to fit or an offshore build nobody can maintain afterwards. Both leave the client worse off than before.

Being in Coimbatore matters to how we work. Clients can meet the people writing the code. We can sit in an office for a week during discovery, which is usually worth more than a month of calls.

We stay a services business rather than chasing a product, because the work we're best at is understanding one organisation's problem properly and building exactly that. There's no roadmap we're trying to fit you onto.

What we believe

01

Say what a thing does, not what it sounds like. Plain language in proposals, in code review, and on this website.

02

Hand over everything. Repository, pipeline, docs, credentials. If you can't leave us, we haven't finished.

03

Senior people on the actual work. No pyramid where the demo team isn't the build team.

04

Say no to work we'd do badly. A referral costs us one project and earns the next three.
